Our Mission

We are Invention Corps of Berkeley. As a collective, we are an interdisciplinary group of students driven to create meaningful global impact through human-centered design. We partner with people and organizations with the objective of creating tangible solutions in the realms of society, health, poverty, and the environment.

Our club hosts a semesterly showcase to share the work we have done on our projects typically with presentations and live demos. This semester, we present our **Virtual Showcase**. Our theme this semester is *String Theory*. As the pandemic has forced many of us apart, we were inspired by the natural forces keeping us together, which you'll see our interpretations of throughout the website.

To adapt our tradition of creating a large-scale physical art piece, we were challenged to reimagine something virtually collaborative and engaging. Deriving inspiration from the idea of the necessary and inevitable reaction of particles, we created a structure of interacting, free-form 'blobs'. Each blob was designed individually by each of our amazingly unique members, visually created as a representation of themselves and the color that represents them.
